# 🎭 Mask of Memories

## A TOBB ETÜ Jam Project
### Story & Inspiration

Mask of Memories is a psychological thriller that explores the invisible layers of the soul through the relationship between Cain and Lyra.

The narrative begins as Cain discovers a mysterious mask in a dusty warehouse, triggering a chronological journey through his most vulnerable and happiest moments.

### Gameplay

- Narrative Platformer: Players guide Cain along a path where memories manifest progressively as he walks.

- Visual Atmosphere: Each scene uses a unique color palette to reflect the specific emotional weight of that memory.

- Exploration: A focused 2D side-scrolling experience designed to build a deep connection between the player and the characters.
